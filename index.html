Caring. Knowledgable. Thorough.

We understand how quickly life can change after a personal injury. You’re left navigating a complex maze of medical and legal systems, all while dealing with the daily pain of recovering from a serious injury. The mental and physical stress is enough to overwhelm any of us. When you come to our firm, we want to be your first step in returning to normality. We guarantee that we will work hard—for you and with you—and we will provide sound, objective advice, and share in the decision-making.

A dedicated team that supports you.

When we accept a case our entire team works collectively, each person brining their unique expertise and background, to make sure we give you the best representation possible. We schedule regular meetings to review our progress with you and to make sure we are informed about your recovery. For payment, our firm works on a contingecy basis—this means that you don’t pay up front, instead we are paid a fixed percentage of the final recovery.
